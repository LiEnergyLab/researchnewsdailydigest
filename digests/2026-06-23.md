**Score:** 10/10 · **Tag:** water electrolysis · **Journal:** OpenAlex · **Published:** 2027-01-01 · **Authors:** James J. C. Counter

Two specialized working electrode assemblies enabled operando XPS and NEXAFS to track iridium oxide catalyst evolution during the oxygen evolution reaction, revealing that amorphous and semi-crystalline oxides form mixed Ir⁴⁺/Ir⁵⁺ states and coordinatively unsaturated oxyl species.
