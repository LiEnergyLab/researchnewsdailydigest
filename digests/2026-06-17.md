### [A new spin on CO 2 reduction](https://www.nature.com/articles/s44286-026-00409-9)
**Score:** 10/10 · **Tag:** CO2 reduction · **Journal:** Nature Chemical Engineering · **Published:** 2026-06-15 · **Authors:** Yanfei Zhu

### [Regulation of H+ transfer pathways promotes C-C coupling in acidic CO2 electroreduction](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-026-74410-4)
**Score:** 10/10 · **Tag:** CO2 electroreduction · **Journal:** OpenAlex · **Published:** 2026-06-15 · **Authors:** Qi Jin, Ye Yang, Dongao Zhang, Xiaodong Yi, Guoxiong Wang, Zhou Chen

A hybrid catalyst combining copper oxide nanosheets with an immobilized cobalt porphyrin achieves up to 89.5% Faradaic efficiency for multi-carbon products from CO₂ at current densities above 1 A cm⁻² in strongly acidic electrolyte (pH 2 and even pH 0.7).
