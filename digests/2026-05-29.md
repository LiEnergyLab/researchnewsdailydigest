### [Beneficial Reduction of Surface Compositional Complexity in High-Entropy Oxide Oxygen Evolution Reaction Catalysts into a Ternary Phase](https://doi.org/10.26434/chemrxiv.15003960/v1)

During OER operation, the surface of a high-entropy Ni/Co/Fe/Mn/Cr (hydr)oxide catalyst transforms into a simpler ternary Ni/Co/Fe or Ni/Co/Mn phase, and this reduction in compositional complexity is what enables long-term efficient performance. These results show that the active surface is less complex than the bulk, guiding rational design of next-generation OER catalysts.
