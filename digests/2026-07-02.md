### [A new spin on CO 2 reduction](https://www.nature.com/articles/s44286-026-00409-9)
**Score:** 10/10 · **Tag:** CO2 reduction · **Journal:** Nature Chemical Engineering · **Published:** 2026-06-15 · **Authors:** Yanfei Zhu

Spinning the electrolyte during CO₂ electrolysis on a copper catalyst shifts the product selectivity from methane to ethylene, achieving a faradaic efficiency of 72% for ethylene at industrially relevant current densities. This offers a simple, scalable method to tune product distribution without modifying the catalyst or electrolyte composition.
