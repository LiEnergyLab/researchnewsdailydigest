### [A high-energy hydrogen radical initiates efficient electrosynthesis of urea from CO 2 and N 2](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41565-026-02209-x)
**Score:** 10/10 · **Tag:** CO2 electroreduction · **Journal:** Nature Nanotechnology · **Published:** 2026-06-22 · **Authors:** Buxing Han

A CuBi catalyst anchored in an ionic liquid achieves efficient urea electrosynthesis from CO₂ and N₂ by generating high-energy hydrogen radicals (H·) through a relayed electron-transfer mechanism, rather than via conventional proton-coupled steps. The work provides a clear mechanistic pathway to bypass the sluggish kinetics typical of co-reducing CO₂ and N₂.
