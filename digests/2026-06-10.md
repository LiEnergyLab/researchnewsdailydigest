### [How Does Water Dissociation Work in Bipolar Membranes?](https://doi.org/10.1021/jacs.6c05146)

Bipolar membrane water dissociation is accelerated by orders of magnitude when heterogeneous catalysts like metal oxides or graphene oxides are added, not just by the electric field alone.
