### [Hydroxide-Permeable Hafnia–Organic Interlayers Protect Ionomer from Oxygen-Evolving Anodes in Pure-Water Alkaline-Membrane Electrolysis](https://doi.org/10.26434/chemrxiv.15002268/v1)

### [Local pH control for impure-water-fed bipolar-membrane electrolyzers](https://doi.org/10.26434/chemrxiv.15002266/v1)

Local pH gradients at the electrode–membrane interfaces, driven by salt ions in the feed water, are a primary cause of voltage loss and degradation in bipolar-membrane electrolyzers. This work establishes local pH control as a key design principle for running electrolyzers on impure water feeds.
