### [Revealing Wavelength- and Size-Dependent CO2 Reduction Selectivity via Operando Scanning Photo-Electrochemical Microscopy](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09612v1)
**Score:** 10/10 · **Tag:** CO2 electroreduction · **Journal:** arXiv · **Published:** 2026-06-08 · **Authors:** Fatemeh Kiani, Milad Sabzehparvar, Priscila Vensaus, Elif Nur Dayi, Olga D'Anania, Tarique Anwar, Nuria Lopez, Ravishankar Sundararaman, Giulia Tagliabue

Tuning the wavelength of light from the visible to the near-infrared switches the product of plasmonic CO₂ reduction on gold/p-GaN photocathodes, with higher-energy photons selectively producing CO and lower-energy photons favoring H₂ evolution.
